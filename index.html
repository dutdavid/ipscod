CORPORATE | GROWTH HACKING

Developing Business Technology Professionals with an Innovation and Growth Mindset

The Ipscod Training programs are developed with a unique and practical approach to a standard MBA program. The hands on training are developed to nurture a Corporate Hacking Mindset with innovation at the core. Corporates need professionals who are able to conduct rapid experimentation within various business operations and identify the most effective and efficient methods to accelerate performance and sustain business growth. We therefore have aligned our programs to produce that exact outcome among professionals that go through our programs.


Helping organizations analyze, optimize and automate business operations with the use of digital tools and technologies

Ipscod programs are aimed at revolutionizing the existing traditional approach in business operations and replace them with modern, efficient and cost effective approaches that leverage on cutting edge business technologies such as digital marketing, Blockchain and Smart Project Management. Ipscod therefore offers certification in Digital Marketing, Smart Project Management and Block chain Technology.


IPSCOD PARTNERSHIPS

Institute of iBusiness Council – IIB

Institute of iBusiness Council is the Business Technology division of EC-Council Group dedicated to developing Enterprise Digital Transformation Training and Certification programs for Corporate Growth Hacking. It is the major certification council for:.
  • Project Management
  • Digital Marketing
  • Blockchain Technology
IPSCOD, through these two mega partnerships, has established itself as the go-to institute in Kenya for professional hands-on skills training in IT technologies and in other developmental fields that produce job-ready graduates.

Indian Institute of Hardware Technology of Bangalore, India, a world leader in IT talent transformation in the retail and enterprise space. Through training in high-end IT technologies, IIHT has helped India to develop a highly skilled workforce that is employable globally.
